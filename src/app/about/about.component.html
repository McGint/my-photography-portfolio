Scott Carey

Scott Carey is a photographer from Cork, Ireland, currently living and working in Dublin. Although he is a Software Developer by trade, photography has always been a hobby and a creative outlet for him.

Scott's photographic style centers around analog 35mm photography, with a particular focus on using the Minolta X-700 and Canon AE-1 cameras. He enjoys the tactile process of film photography, and the unique character that each roll of film brings to his images.

Despite his love for film photography, Scott is also proficient in digital photography, and often uses his technical skills to manipulate and enhance his images in post-processing.

When he's not taking photographs, Scott can often be found exploring the streets of Dublin, seeking out new and interesting perspectives to capture through his lens.
